The HLA-B Leader Assessment Tool (BLEAT) translates HLA-B leader matching based off of the research findings of Role of HLA-B exon 1 in graft-versus-host disease after unrelated haemopoietic cell transplantation: a retrospective cohort study (Petersdorf et. al 2020): https://doi.org/10.1016/S2352-3026(19)30208-X

Raise any issues at https://github.com/nmdp-bioinformatics/b-leader/issues.
